Fast algorithms for l-adic towers over finite fields

research paper presented at ISSAC 2013

Download .zip Download .tar.gz View on GitHub

Inspired by previous work of Shoup, Lenstra-De Smit and Couveignes-Lercier, we give fast algorithms to compute in (the first levels of) the $\ell$-adic closure of a finite field. In many cases, our algorithms have quasi-linear complexity.

This paper will be presented at the International Symposium on Symbolic and Algebraic Computation 2013 in Boston.

A preprint version is available at http://arxiv.org/abs/1301.6021.

The GitHub sources contain the LaTeX source code as well as the Sage scripts demonstrating the concepts involved.
